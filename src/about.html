Steve Shelton

Steve is well known in his real estate practice for great customer service, consistent communication, educating clients and ensuring a positive experience for all parties in a transaction. Steve’s approach to every transaction is this “it’s all about the people, not about the agent”. During his 10+yrs. Steve has helped many families with their real estate needs. Steve knows the meaning of communication and knows what it takes to successfully close a transaction making buying, selling or investing a positive and seamless experience. “I’m not #1 YOU are!” Steve also dedicates his time offering volunteer support to three special organizations: SCCA *Seattle Cancer Care Alliance-Events Staff, Colon Stars-booth volunteer to help bring awareness to colon cancer prevention and Watts Basketball Foundation as a basketball coach to help kids develop basketball skills to help empower personal growth.
